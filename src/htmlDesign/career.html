About DeepLink Data Solutions

DeepLink Data Solutions is a cutting-edge technology company specializing in software testing and quality assurance solutions. We help businesses deliver flawless digital experiences through our expertise in automation testing, security testing, AI-powered testing, and performance engineering.

Responsibilities

  • Develop and maintain automated test scripts
  • Work with testing frameworks like Selenium, Appium, or Cypress
  • Participate in test planning and strategy sessions
  • Identify, document, and track software defects
  • Collaborate with development teams to improve product quality

Requirements

  • Pursuing or recently completed degree in Computer Science or related field
  • Basic understanding of software testing concepts
  • Familiarity with any programming language (Java, Python, JavaScript)
  • Strong problem-solving skills
  • Eagerness to learn automation tools and techniques

Nice to Have

  • Experience with version control (Git)
  • Knowledge of CI/CD pipelines
  • Understanding of API testing

Application Process

  1. Submit your application through our online form
  2. Selected candidates will be contacted for an initial screening
  3. Technical interview with our testing team
  4. Final interview with management
  5. Offer extended to successful candidates

We review applications on a rolling basis and encourage early submissions.
