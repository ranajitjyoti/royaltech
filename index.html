Our QA Process

At DeepLink Data Solutions, we believe that quality is not an afterthought—it's built into every stage of development. Our structured QA process combines proven methodologies, cutting‑edge tools, and a deep understanding of client requirements to ensure your product meets the highest standards of functionality, performance, and reliability.

1

Envision

Before we begin any testing engagement, we take the time to thoroughly understand your business objectives, application scope, and quality expectations. Our QA experts assess your current testing maturity level, existing processes, tools, and documentation.

2

Allocation of Team

Once the requirements are clear, we handpick a team of Quality Analysts and Engineers who bring the right domain expertise, technical skill set, and tool proficiency for your project.

3

Project Kick Off

In this stage, we finalize the testing methodology—Agile, Waterfall, Hybrid, or Continuous Testing—based on your delivery model and priorities. We set up the required tools, frameworks, and environments.

4

Exhaustive Reporting

Our reporting process goes beyond just pass/fail metrics. We provide detailed defect analytics, coverage statistics, trend graphs, and actionable insights to help you make informed decisions.
